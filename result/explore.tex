\subsubsection{Iteration 5: Exploration in a Realistic Environment}

\paragraph{Redesign}

Building on the third iteration, an exploration mode within the realistic environment was introduced to support the active experimentation phase of experiential learning. The intention was to allow users to navigate the environment more freely and apply previously acquired piloting skills in a less constrained context.

To evaluate this approach, environmental constraints were reduced by removing the collider boundaries. Participants were assigned a navigation task requiring them to travel independently from Viru Gate to St.\ Olaf's Church, which is the tallest structure in the modeled environment shown in \cref{fig:tallinn}.

\paragraph{Enactment}

The primary technical modification involved the removal of boundary constraints, with no additional interaction mechanics introduced in this phase.
