\section{Results}

The quadcopter physics simulation in this study is implemented based on an existing FPV physics model \parencite{yuetility2022fpvphysics}. As the physics model is adopted from prior work, the primary focus of the results is on how the model is integrated with XR controllers and interaction mechanisms.

This chapter presents two main components: (1) the iterative development of XR interaction, and (2) the iterative development of the Human-AI collaboration agent. The results of both components are presented following iterative cycles of design, enactment, analysis, and redesign, as outlined in the DBR framework.
